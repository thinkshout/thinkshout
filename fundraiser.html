ThinkShout has moved...but not far! We're maintaining our Old Town roots, and as a sign of good faith to the neighboring organizations in the area, we're making our open house a fundraiser benefiting the following groups:

  • Blanchet House
  • Transition Projects
  • Sisters of the Road

There will be music, food, drinks, and views of downtown Portland to enjoy from the 7th floor of 4th and Glisan. Ticket costs benefit the organizations listed above. You may also make a supplemental donation to the organization of your choosing (which you can select on the billing info page!).

More About Us

ThinkShout is a full service digital agency (and certified B Corp) that focuses on serving nonprofits, universities, and healthcare organizations to help them achieve maximum impact. We do this through compelling strategic solutions, accessible design, and open source technologies. We also offer scalable website solutions for smaller organizations, as well as dedicated fundraising and strategic services so that you can effectively tell your story and engage your constituents in meaningful ways.

We have distilled the following vision for our business:

  • Invest in the most talented technologists, project managers, and designers possible.
  • Work with clients committed to positive social change.
  • Don't compromise on tools, process, or quality.
  • Work hard, have fun, and give back.
