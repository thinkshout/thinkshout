Meet Bene, a complete website solution for growing nonprofits.

Bene is an open source platform that bundles your website’s content management with strategic support that focuses on your organization’s specific needs.

And because Bene is open source, it means you’re getting a flexible, responsive site that will evolve in complexity as your organization does. As contributions and enhancements are made to the product, you’ll have the ability to apply those enhancements to your site with minimum fuss.
